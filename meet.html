meet the ewes

cheeks

Cheeks aka the best sheep. Always the first to come over and will follow you around, well, like a sheep. She is very sweet natured and just loves fuss and attention with also a cheeky side to her personality!

lop-ear

Can ewe guess where she got the name from? Her lop sided ears make her absolutely adorable. She is always intrigued to know what's going on and her ears make her look particularly derpy whilst doing so.

tufty

Let's just say she always has a bad hair day. You can't help but smile when you see Tufty because of her crazy fluff. Such a patient and lovely sheep who you can spot from a mile away.

sunshine

Cheeks' best pal - they are literally inseparable. I can guarantee if Cheeks is there, Sunshine will be close behind. She is a super confident girl who will climb up anything just for some attention.

dorothy

Dorothy has the shortest legs you ever did see but that makes her super cute. She will waddle over to you just to say hi. An sweet girl with a kind heart.

× Dorothy lambed on 5th March - two gorgeous twins!

wispy

Wispy is a fairly confident sheep with a whole lot of sass. She will push her way through to the front with no trouble, in hope that you might feed her first.

stirrup

Stirrup was a sheep who was super shy, she really wanted to know what was going on but was far too nervous. Her confidence has now grown and it is lovely to see that she now enjoys a head pat.

lamble

Technically not an ewe but still part of the squad. She was last years' orphan lamb. I claimed her and now she is my big baby. Loves humans, fuss, cuddles and food!! Click here to watch her grow up.
